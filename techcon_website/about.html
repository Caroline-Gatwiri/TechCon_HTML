Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of software developers in Austin, Texas, passionate about open-source technology and innovation.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire innovation, foster global collaboration, and empower technologists to build a better future. We believe technology should be inclusive, ethical, and driven by human impact — not just profit or trends.

By creating a space where researchers, engineers, entrepreneurs, and students can share ideas freely, we aim to accelerate progress while promoting diversity, accessibility, and sustainability in tech.

Past Featured Speakers

Dr. Amara Patel – AI Ethicist

Chief AI Ethicist at FutureMind Labs, Dr. Patel delivered a groundbreaking keynote at TechCon 2021, challenging the industry to address bias in machine learning. Her research has influenced policies at major tech companies worldwide.

Jordan Lee – Green Tech Pioneer

Founder of the GreenCode Initiative, Jordan led a workshop in 2022 on reducing the carbon footprint of software. His tools help developers optimize energy efficiency without sacrificing performance.
